# Direction 8A: RH as arithmetic Rosati positivity (the standard-conjecture form of the signature)

> A sharpening of Direction 8, written 2026-05-30 after the cross-direction synthesis ([`../all_roads_to_the_signature.md`](../all_roads_to_the_signature.md)) localized RH as a signature/positivity. This note puts that positivity in its standard professional form and names the precise theorem to prove. Computational anchor: [`experiments/arithmetic_geometric/e2t_rosati_positivity.py`](../../../experiments/arithmetic_geometric/e2t_rosati_positivity.py).
>
> **Headline.** "Hodge index on a constructed surface" is one phrasing of the target. The sharper, more standard phrasing is the **positivity of a Rosati involution** on the Frobenius endomorphism algebra, equivalently an **arithmetic Hodge standard conjecture** for the Deninger Frobenius correspondence. This is the actual engine of Weil 1948, needs no ambient surface to state, and places RH inside Grothendieck's standard-conjectures circle, which is its correct research home.

## 1. The reformulation

Weil's proof of RH for a curve $C/\mathbb{F}_q$ does not, at its core, invoke a surface. It invokes the **positivity of the Rosati involution**: on the endomorphism algebra $\mathrm{End}^0(\mathrm{Jac}\,C) \otimes \mathbb{R}$, the trace form
$$B(x,y) = \mathrm{Tr}(x\, y^\dagger), \qquad x^\dagger = \text{Rosati adjoint w.r.t. the canonical polarization},$$
is **positive definite**. Applied to Frobenius $\pi$ (with $\pi^\dagger = \bar\pi$, $\pi\bar\pi = q$), positivity forces $|\alpha_i| = \sqrt q$ for every eigenvalue of $\pi$ on $H^1$, which is RH for $C$. The Hodge index on $C\times C$ is the *geometric incarnation* of the same positivity (the intersection form on the correspondence part of $\mathrm{NS}(C\times C)$ is minus the Rosati form), but the Rosati form is the primary object.

**This is verified, fully, in [`e2t`](../../../experiments/arithmetic_geometric/e2t_rosati_positivity.py).** Across genus 1-2 curves over $\mathbb{F}_q$, the Rosati Gram $G[a][b] = q^{\min(a,b)} a_{|a-b|}$ (with $a_k = \sum_i\alpha_i^k = q^k+1-N_k$) has no negative eigenvalues, and that is equivalent to $|\alpha_i|=\sqrt q$. (Zero eigenvalues occur exactly when $\pi$ generates a proper subalgebra, e.g. genus-2 over $\mathbb{F}_5$: the form is positive *semi*-definite, rank $=\dim\mathbb{Q}(\pi)$.) For $g=1$, $G=\begin{pmatrix}2&t\\ t&2q\end{pmatrix}$, positive definite $\Leftrightarrow |t|<2\sqrt q$, which is $-1$ times the 2G primitive intersection Gram. Four equivalent faces of RH-for-$C$ are now in the record: Rosati positivity (2T), primitive intersection negative-definiteness (2G), $|\alpha_i|=\sqrt q$, and the TP/flow zeros on $\mathrm{Re}(s)=1/2$ (2S).

## 2. The arithmetic target, stated precisely

RH for $\zeta$ is the arithmetic analogue:

> **Target (arithmetic Rosati positivity).** There is a positive Rosati-type involution $\dagger$ on the "arithmetic Frobenius algebra" $\mathcal{A}$ of $\mathrm{Spec}(\mathbb{Z})$ -- the (place-graded) algebra generated by the Deninger Frobenius correspondence $\Gamma_S$ -- such that the trace form $B(x,y)=\mathrm{Tr}(x\,y^\dagger)$ is positive (no negative spectrum) on $\mathcal{A}\otimes\mathbb{R}$. RH is this positivity.

The ingredients are pinned by the project's own findings:
- $\Gamma_S$ has place-dependent bidegree $(1,p)$ (LEARNINGS #25), so $\mathcal{A}$ is graded over the places, scale $p$ in the $p$-block.
- The trace form's "diagonal" is the regularized self-intersection $\Gamma_S^2 = \sum_p (\log p)(\cdots)$, the von Mangoldt prime sum (LEARNINGS #26).
- The archimedean place must enter the positivity together with the finite places (the two-clock balance, LEARNINGS #18/#23): positivity is global, not place-by-place.

## 3. Why this is the right professional framing

- **It is a standard conjecture.** Over a field, the positivity of the Rosati form is the **Hodge standard conjecture** (one of Grothendieck's standard conjectures), a theorem in char $0$ via Hodge theory and the Hodge-Riemann relations, open in general char. RH-for-$\zeta$ is its arithmetic ($\mathbb{F}_1$/$\mathrm{Spec}\,\mathbb{Z}$) analogue. This is not a fringe reformulation; it is the Serre-Grothendieck view of the Weil conjectures applied to the arithmetic case, and it connects the program to a large, active literature (standard conjectures, motives, polarizations, Hodge-Riemann).
- **No ambient surface required.** We do not need to construct $\mathrm{Spec}(\mathbb{Z})\times_{\mathbb{F}_1}\mathrm{Spec}(\mathbb{Z})$ to *state* the target. We need the Frobenius algebra $\mathcal{A}$ and a polarization (a $\dagger$ with $B$ positive). That is a lighter, more attackable object than a surface.
- **It is non-circular (K1).** Over $\mathbb{F}_q$ the Rosati positivity comes from the *polarization* (a geometric positivity), and RH is its consequence; it is not read off the zeros. The arithmetic target asks for the same: a polarization-type positivity, not a trace identity. This is precisely the R3.5-escaping signature route.
- **K2 holds.** Davenport-Heilbronn has no Euler product, hence no Frobenius, hence no Frobenius algebra $\mathcal{A}$ to polarize. The target is unbuildable for the counterexample.

## 4. The research program (concrete milestones)

This replaces "20-50 postdoc-years, probability <1%" with a sequence of attackable theorems. We are a research group; we prove these in order.

- **M1 (done, 2T).** Verify the Rosati formulation in the function-field case and its equivalence to the three other faces. Establish the sign-flip to the intersection form. *Status: complete, exact, genus 1-2.*
- **M2.** Construct the arithmetic Frobenius algebra $\mathcal{A}$ as a concrete (place-graded) $\mathbb{R}$-algebra with involution, using the known data: the $(1,p)$ bidegree blocks (#25), the von Mangoldt diagonal (#26), and the single-arithmetic-surface Arakelov/Faltings-Hriljac pairings already computed (2H-2P). Even a *finite-prime truncation* $\mathcal{A}_P$ with the archimedean block is a genuine object whose trace-form signature can be computed and whose stabilization as $P\to\infty$ can be studied. *Status: ATTEMPTED ([e2u](../../../experiments/arithmetic_geometric/e2u_rosati_balance_M2.py), LEARNINGS #32). The form is correctly assembled on non-circular data (the 3M place-type split) as the archimedean-dominance balance, but the verdict is CONTAMINATED by the archimedean-block fidelity: the balance comes out backwards (D-H outranks zeta), confirming the #20 A_arch offset independently. Gate identified; see M2.5.*
- **M2.5 (the named gate).** Recompute the archimedean block $A_{\mathrm{arch}}$ from the bilinear Bombieri physical-space integral. *Status: DONE for $\zeta$ ([e2v](../../../experiments/arithmetic_geometric/e2v_rosati_balance_M2_5.py), LEARNINGS #33). Ported 3F's validated archimedean integral into the matrix as a cross-b bilinear block; VALIDATED by $T_{\max}$-convergence (the residual against the zero-side Gram $\to 0$ as $T_{\max}\to\infty$, proving the block exact; the #32 "contamination" was the zero-side truncation). Corrected the diagnostic to $\min\mathrm{eig}(A_{\mathrm{arch}}+P_{\mathrm{fin}}+B_{\mathrm{pole}})$ (the pencil "balance" presumed $A_{\mathrm{arch}}$ PD, which it is not). RESULT: $\min\mathrm{eig} = +0.035 > 0$ (stable across $K=5..8$) -- the non-circular reconstructed Weil form for $\zeta$ is positive, a non-circular positivity certificate (necessary-not-sufficient; the finite-$K$ truncation of the target). D-H's $\mu=1$ kernel is mis-normalized (deferred to M2.6).*
- **M2.6 (immediate next).** Derive and validate the general-$\mu$ archimedean Bombieri kernel ($\mu=1$ for D-H, $\mu\in\{0,1\}$ for Epstein) so the controls get trustworthy $\min\mathrm{eig}(M)$. Then the four-way RH-vs-Euler verdict is decisive: does $\min\mathrm{eig}(M)>0$ hold for $\zeta$ and Epstein-principal (RH) and fail for D-H (RH-fails)? If yes, the certificate separates RH from non-Euler-ness (defeats the #20 trap). *Status: open.*
- **M3.** Identify the **polarization**: the geometric source of positivity over $\mathbb{Z}$. In char $0$ it is the Hodge-Riemann form; over $\mathbb{Z}$ the candidate is the Arakelov/archimedean Green's-function pairing (the 2I-2L Petersson piece) glued to the finite places. Prove (or find the obstruction to) positivity of $B$ from this polarization on $\mathcal{A}_P$.
- **M4.** Pass to the limit $P\to\infty$: control the archimedean-dominates-prime balance (the hard core, #20/3M) and prove positivity survives. This is the arithmetic Hodge standard conjecture.
- **M5.** Derive RH for $\zeta$ from M4 (the eigenvalue/zero consequence), and verify K1/K2/K3.

M2 and M3 are reachable now and are the immediate work. M4 is the deep step. M5 is bookkeeping once M4 lands.

## 5. The immediate next experiment

Build $\mathcal{A}_P$: the truncated arithmetic Frobenius trace form on the first $P$ primes plus the archimedean place, with entries assembled from the von Mangoldt diagonal (#26) and the Arakelov data (2H-2P), and compute its signature. The professional questions: does it have no negative eigenvalues (positivity), does the signature stabilize as $P$ grows, and does the D-H analogue acquire a negative eigenvalue (K2)? This is milestone M2 and it is concrete, finite, and runnable.
