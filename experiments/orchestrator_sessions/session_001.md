## Recommended deployment for session 002

### Primary: three BUILDERs in parallel on Direction 1 milestone 4.1

Per [`builder.md`](../../.claude/agents/builder.md) "Multi-agent parallel construction" pattern, deploy three BUILDER instances on the same target with different angles:

- **BUILDER-1A**: blueprint-relation form. Define psi_p(x) - x^p as an element of the blueprint's relation ideal. Specify which generators of the relation ideal are needed for Fermat-Frobenius to specialize correctly to Lambda-Rings.
- **BUILDER-1B**: Adams-operation form. Lift {psi_p} from ring endomorphisms on (B, B-bullet) and require psi_p(x) congruent to x^p in the quotient B / I_p where I_p is the blueprint ideal generated by p.
- **BUILDER-1C**: delta-ring lifted form. Define delta_p(x) = (psi_p(x) - x^p)/p in the blueprint setting, working modulo torsion. Use the R5 connection: delta-rings are Lambda-rings at one prime per [`2A_R5_prismatic_cohomology.md`](../arithmetic_geometric/2A_R5_prismatic_cohomology.md).

Each BUILDER writes its candidate definition + verification of:
- Specialization to Lambda-Rings (recovers Joyal's definition when blueprint is a ring).
- Specialization to Lorscheid blueprints with trivial psi (recovers the parent category).
- Worked example: F_1 trivially, Z with psi_p = id by Fermat's little theorem.
- Self-assessment against the 17-constraint framework.

Output target: `experiments/arithmetic_geometric/D1_4_1_fermat_frobenius_candidate_{A,B,C}.md`.

**Success criteria**:
- All three candidates produced.
- Each has a precise definition stateable as a Lean structure extending the existing [`LambdaBlueprints.lean`](../../lean/ZetaRH/LambdaBlueprints.lean) skeleton.
- Each has worked examples computed explicitly for F_1 and Z.
- At least one candidate passes the specialization check.

**Falsifiability for this sub-task**: if all three candidates fail one of the specialization checks (do not recover Lambda-Rings OR do not recover Blpr), Direction 1 milestone 4.1 is in trouble. Pivot to deploying Direction 2 (Borger+Connes) BUILDERs in parallel.
